Condition-Specific Guidance

General Wellness Plan

Exercise

  • 150 minutes moderate exercise weekly
  • 2 days strength training
  • Daily stretching/mobility

Nutrition

  • 5+ servings fruits/vegetables daily
  • Limit processed foods
  • Stay hydrated (8 glasses water)

Medical

  • Annual physical exam
  • Dental cleaning every 6 months
  • Vision exam every 2 years

Doctor Consultation Guide

When to See a Doctor

  • Persistent symptoms lasting >2 weeks
  • Severe pain or discomfort
  • Unexplained weight changes

Questions to Ask

  • What screenings do I need based on family history?
  • Are there preventive medications I should consider?
  • What lifestyle changes would most benefit me?
